1. The Hive Hotel
Featuring a shared rooftop terrace, The Hive Hotel is 300 m from Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica. The hotel provides free WiFi throughout and air- conditioned rooms, all rooms at this property have a modern-style décor, and come with a flat-screen TV. Each room has a private bathroom with slippers, toiletries and a hairdryer, while some also include a seating area, an extensive sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily. Guests can also find a bar and a restaurant serving Italian and Chinese cuisine.
