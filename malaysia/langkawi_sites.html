Sites - Langkawi

Island Hopping

Langkawi Island Hopping is ideal for anyone who wants to enjoy the wildlife, dense forest, freshwaters, and picturesque views on their island tour. Visit three picturesque islands located in the south of Langkawi's main island, such as Pregnant Maiden Island, Singa Besar Island, and Beras Basah Island. Witness eagle feeds in which eagles swoop down from the sky to catch their prey on the water.

Cable Car

Step aboard the Langkawi SkyCab cable car and ride to the second highest peak of the islands at the top of Mt. Machinchang. During the ascent over the jungle, look down and see the geologic formations that date back about 550 million years.

Buy your tickets here.

Underwater World Langkawi

Explore the the unique Underwater World Langkawi with its gigantic, 15-meter long, walk-through tunnel consisting of 600,000 liters of seawater and houses huge fish and sea creatures. Other attractions include the Freshwater Fish Section, the Seashell Display, the Koi Pond, the Reptilian Section, and the Coral Reef Section.
