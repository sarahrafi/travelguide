Aquaria KLCC

Aquaria KLCC has an amazing showcase of 5,000 aquatic creature exhibits spread over 60,000 square feet of space. For starters, they have seven species of sharks such as the fearsome sand tiger sharks, blacktip and white tip reef sharks, zebra sharks, tawny nurse sharks, wobbegong sharks, and brownbanded bamboo sharks

KL Bird Park

The Bird Park is set in a sprawling valley and is home to more than 3,000 birds. We spent a great 2 hours here, exploring the different areas and walking through the "world's largest free-flight aviary" to see the birds up close. There is pretty much 1 route around the park, so very easy to follow.
