Sites - Lyon

Museum of Fine Arts

One of the most important tourist places in Lyon is an important cultural heritage of the city, and it is the second most important museum in France after the Louvre. It is located in a palace founded in the 16th century, belonging to St. Pierre. The museum occupies one of the largest collections of works of art in Europe, which includes artifacts, paintings, sculptures and ornamental works belonging to ancient Egypt.

Teatro Galo-Romano

The Ancient Theatre of Fourvière is a Roman theatre in Lyon, France. It was built on the hill of Fourvière, which is located in the center of the Roman city. The theatre is part of a UNESCO World Heritage Site protecting the historic center of Lyon.

Golden Cape Garden

Golden Cape Park is located in the city center, on the north bank of the Rhône River. It has a zoo, a botanical garden, a rose garden, and a lake.Families can sail by boat and stroll through these picturesque natural spaces.
