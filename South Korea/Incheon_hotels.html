Hotels - Incheon
A rich combination of past, present and future
1. Paradise City
Paradise City features an indoor and outdoor pool open all year round as well as a fitness centre and a children's playground. Free WiFi access and free private parking is available at the hotel. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a complimentary minibar. En suite bathroom is fitted with a bath and a toilet with an electronic bidet. Extras include slippers, a bathrobe, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

    he 5-star Sheraton Grand Incheon Hotel features luxurious rooms in Songdo-Incheon. Boasting an indoor pool, fitness centre and spa, the non-smoking hotel has two restaurants, a lobby lounge and a bar. Sheraton Grand Incheon Hotel is a 2-minute walk to the Songdo Convention Centre. Central Park is an 8-minute walk away while it takes 15 minutes to walk to University of Incheon Station (Incheon Subway Line 1). Incheon International Airport is a 35-minute drive from the hotel.
