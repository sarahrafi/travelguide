lotte world

Lotte World - a gaming complex in the capital of South Korea, Seoul, and it consists of a large hall that contains an indoor games park, in addition to an outdoor park, hotels and shopping stores, a Korean folk museum, sports facilities and cinemas, and Lotte World is one of the largest game parks In the world, it ranks seventh in the world according to the statistics of the year2002

Gangnam

Gangnam-gu is the chic and modern center of Seoul, famous for its gleaming skyscrapers, designer boutiques and chic nightclubs where top DJs deliver techno and house beats. The restaurants range from high-end restaurants to bars that serve fried chicken and soju rice wine. The COEX Mall attracts visitors with its aquarium, while the Seoul Arts Center houses a design museum and theatre.

Namsan Tower

The N Seoul Tower or YTN Seoul Tower, commonly known as Namsan Tower or Seoul Tower, is officially a communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in the center of the capital, Seoul, in South Korea. The tower is the second highest point in Seoul, with a height of 236 meters
